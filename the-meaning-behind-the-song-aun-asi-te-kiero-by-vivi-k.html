The Meaning Behind The Song: An as te kiero by Vivi K

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 8, 2024

Introduction

As a music enthusiast, I always find joy in stumbling upon new songs that resonate with me on a deeper level. One such song that has recently caught my attention is “Aún así te kiero” by Vivi K. The combination of Vivi K’s mesmerizing voice and the heartfelt lyrics immediately captured my emotions.

My First Encounter with the Song

I remember stumbling upon this song at a friend’s house. It was playing softly in the background, and I couldn’t help but be drawn into its captivating melody. As the lyrics began to fill the room, I found myself feeling a mixture of happiness and sadness, as if the song was speaking directly to my own experiences.

The Lyrics and Their Meaning

“Aún así te kiero” translates to “Still, I love you” in English. The lyrics beautifully express the complexities of a relationship and the unwavering love that can exist even in the face of challenges and pain. In the first verse, Vivi K sings, “Ojalá que mi silencio haga ruido en tu cabeza” which means “I hope that my silence makes noise in your head.” It portrays a longing for the other person to understand their feelings without explicitly expressing them.

The chorus further emphasizes the strength of love and loyalty, even in the face of material possessions. Vivi K says, “Haciéndolo encima de ese dinero” which translates to “Doing it on top of that money.” It highlights the idea that their love is not influenced or overshadowed by material wealth.

The second verse delves into introspection and personal growth. The lines “Miro para arriba ya me siento bien, Sé que fui tu ruina, Sé que soy tu sed” meaning “I look up, I feel good now. I know I was your ruin, I know I am your thirst” depict a sense of self-realization and acceptance.

Personal Connection

This song, for me, resonates with past relationships and the rollercoaster of emotions that come with them. It reminds me of how love can be incredibly powerful, even in the most challenging times. It speaks to the inner conflicts we all face when trying to navigate the complexities of love and the deep-rooted desire to make relationships work.

As I listen to “Aún así te kiero,” I can’t help but think of the moments where I have chosen to stay and love someone despite the hardships we faced. It serves as a reminder of the strength we possess when it comes to matters of the heart.

In conclusion, “Aún así te kiero” by Vivi K is a beautiful song that speaks to the depths of love and the intricacies of relationships. Through its heartfelt lyrics and enchanting melody, it captures the essence of enduring love and remaining committed despite the challenges. This song has left a lasting impact on me, reminding me of the resilience and power that love holds within our lives.
