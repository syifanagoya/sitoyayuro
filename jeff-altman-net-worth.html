What is Jeff Altman's Net Worth?

Jeff Altman is an American stand-up comedian and actor who has a net worth of $1.5 million. Jeff Altman was born in Syracuse, New York in August 1951. Altman has appeared on the Late Show with David Letterman over 40 times. He first appeared on the TV series Mary Hartman, Mary Hartman and Cos in 1976. From 1979 to 1985 he had a recurring role as Hughie Hogg on the TV series The Dukes of Hazzard. From 1988 to 1989 Altman provided the voice of Professional Normal Dweeb on the series Slimer!

And the Real Ghostbusters. He starred as Greg Vincent on the television series Nurses from 1991 to 1992. Altman had a recurring role on the TV series Baywatch from 1989 to 1998. He also starred in the movies American Hot Wax, Wacko, Doin' Time, Soul Man, Highlander II: The Quickening, Russian Holiday, Back by Midnight, and Urban Decay. He is also an accomplished magician and a card man. Altman was a spokesman for Arby's restaurants and hosted a defensive driving course for Blockbuster Video.
