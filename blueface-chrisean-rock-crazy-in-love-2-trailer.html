Chrisean Rock's Pregnancy Featured In 'Crazy In Love' 2 Trailer

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Blueface and Chrisean Rock have one of the more dramatic — and, arguably, toxic — relationships in Hip Hop.

But, bad relationships make for good television, and that’s exactly what the couple is banking on as they promote the latest season of the Zeus Network’s show, Crazy in Love, whose second season will feature their pregnancy journey so far.

AD LOADING...

“@chriseanrockbabyy and @bluefasebabyy’s, SEASON 2 #CrazyInLove is hitting our screens THIS JULY!!” read the network’s Instagram caption for the teaser, which dropped on Sunday (July 2). “From #ChriseanRock’s pregnancy journey, family drama to sold out crowds all while being crazy in love and experiencing the ups and downs in their relationship! Who’s ready to tune into the Premiere of SEASON 2 #CrazyInLove?! Drop those in the comments.”

Check out the teaser trailer below.

The on-again, off-again couple have had their fair share of ups and downs.

Last month, Chrisean Rock made headlines when she referred to Blueface as “the Devil” after he got arrested in Las Vegas.

The “Thotiana” rapper was arrested and hit with a felony robbery charge in Las Vegas on June 7 following a court appearance he had for an entirely different charge.

Blueface was taken into police custody while he appeared in court for a preliminary hearing for his attempted murder charges.

While details were scarce, Blue’s lawyers informed TMZ: “We look forward to vigorously defending him on both cases.”

While Chrisean Rock was publicly silent on her baby father’s arrest, an affiliate of hers hopped on Instagram Live, and eagle-eyed fans were able to hear Rock’s voice in the background of the video, where she said: “Glad I ain’t go home, they got him…Good, ’cause that n-gga the Devil.”

Blueface caught wind of his on-again, off-again girlfriend’s comments and responded via Instagram after he was released from police custody.

“Y’all can believe the sob story she tell y’all everyday all day,” he wrote on his IG Story. “Only time will tell who was really solid. I know the bih an she deserve everything I got for her [eggplant emoji].”

In a separate video, Blue added: “I heard what the bitch said. She said, ‘Are you on Live? Oh shit, they heard me say all of that weird-ass shit!’”
