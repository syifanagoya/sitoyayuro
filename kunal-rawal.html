Fashion Designing Career & Professions

Kunal Rawal got interested in fashion design during his school time. After obtaining a degree in fashion designing from London College of Fashion, he worked for the British automobile company, Ginetta and designed apparel and accessories for them.

Afterward, he moved back to India and started working on his own. He established his first company named ‘dstress’ in 2006. He started offering a wide range of menswear. Additionally, he also designed clothes for IPL teams such as Royal Challengers Bangalore, Rajasthan Royals, and others.

Afterward, he started his label ‘Kunal Rawal’ and began providing knotting techniques, pleating, metallic work, appliquéing, embroidery, and many more. His collections are always unique and up to mark for the client. Afterward, he began designing for renowned Bollywood actors, businessmen, and other people. He has collaborated with Varun Dhawan, Prateik Babbar, Arjun Kapoor, Ranbir Kapoor, Anil Kapoor, Shahid Kapoor, Siddharth Malhotra, designer Michael Russo, comedian Hasan Minhaj, and many more.
