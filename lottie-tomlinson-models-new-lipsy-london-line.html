Lottie Tomlinson Models New Lipsy London Line

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

Less than two weeks ago, Lottie Tomlinson posted photos on her Instagram page featuring a photo shoot, but this time instead of being in charge of the behind the scenes look – Lottie was front and center, modeling for Lipsy London!

Fans of Lottie thought there might be a bit of a wait before they found out what she was up to, but within days it was revealed that she had modeled Ariana’s new line for Lipsy and the photos from the shoot are absolutely stunning!

The  looks are a mix of sassy, classic, sexy, and fun all in one.  From lace to crop tops and bodycon dresses – there’s something to fit anyone’s style and the looks are totally affordable, we’re already ordering a lot of it!

While some people think Lottie’s claim to fame was due in large to her brother, Louis Tomlinson, the truth is she worked her way from the bottom up!  She revealed in an interview earlier this year that she asked already established fashion goddess, Lou Teasdale if she could work alongside her and she definitely earned her dues.  Lottie not only does incredible work on the artists she styles, she is absolutely gorgeous herself so she can wear anything and rock any makeup look and have us all wanting to try it!

Lottie has been working hard in 2016 – partnering with Lipsy, The Book Channel, Ghost Fragrances, and she will be part of Selena’s beauty team on her upcoming tour!  When it comes to working hard for her dreams, Lottie knows how to do it!  She’s young, driven, and talented – and with a supportive family at her side, there’s absolutely no stopping her!

If you want to see more looks from the Lipsy line, more of her beauty and style tips, and some awesome snaps of her life – complete with adorable pics of her beau – follow her on Instagram and check out Lipsy London!
