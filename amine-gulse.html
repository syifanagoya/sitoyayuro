Amine Gülşe is a Swedish-Turkish actress, model, and beauty pageant titleholder who is best known for having been crowned Miss Turkey 2014. She has also appeared in a couple of TV shows and has garnered a huge social media fan base as well and enjoys more than 3 million followers on Instagram, more than 500k followers on Facebook, and more than 20k followers on Twitter.

    Amine Gülşe Facts

  • She grew up in Gothenburg, Sweden along with her brother.
  • Her mother hails from İzmir, Turkey, and her father is from Kirkuk, Iraq.
  • In order to pursue a career as an actress as well as a model, Amine Gülşe moved to Istanbul, Turkey.
  • She emerged as the winner of Miss Turkey 2014 and was crowned as Miss World Turkey. A year later, Amine Gülşe crowned the volleyball player, model, and beauty pageant titleholder, Ecem Cirpan, as her successor.
  • Miss Turkey 2014 pageant was conducted at the Star TV Studios in Istanbul on 27th May.
  • Amine Gülşe also participated in Miss World 2014. Her competitor for the competition was none other than the model and winner, Rolene Strauss.
  • In the year 2015, she was cast in a leading role in the television series, Asla Vazgeçmem.
  • She appeared as a guest in an episode of the television talk-show, Dada Dandinista, in May 2015.
  • Amine Gülşe played the character of Nur Kozan/Nur in the drama TV series, Never Give Up, from 2015 to 2016.
  • She can speak multiple languages such as Turkish, English, Swedish, and Spanish.
  • Amine Gülşe shares a deep bond with her mother and has uploaded a number of their pictures on Instagram.
